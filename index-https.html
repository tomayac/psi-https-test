Brainly is the world’s largest social learning community for students.

Our mission is to inspire students to share and explore knowledge in a collaborative community.

The place to learn

Brainly brings high school and middle school students together to make learning outside the classroom highly engaging, effective and rewarding. Students connect to their peers to help strengthen their skills, from math, to science, to history and beyond.
